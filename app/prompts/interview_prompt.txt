[TIER_BASIC]

- Pull questions DIRECTLY from their listed skills and resume experience. Ask about things they claim to know.
- Each question must cover a COMPLETELY DIFFERENT skill or technology — never repeat the same topic.
- Keep it beginner-friendly, practical, and encouraging.
- Use EASY difficulty only.
- Example style: "You listed Python on your resume — can you explain how you would use list comprehensions?"

[TIER_GAP_ANALYSIS]
GAP ANALYSIS TIER (score 60-79): The candidate has a moderate match.
